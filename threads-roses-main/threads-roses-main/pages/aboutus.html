Our Story

Founded in 2024, Threads & Roses began as a small local thrift initiative built on one belief that every piece of clothing has a story worth sharing. From timeless vintage finds to one-of-a-kind modern pieces, we bring you sustainable fashion without compromise.

We believe that there is change coming in the fashion industry, that consumers are more concerned with where their clothes come from and the true cost of wearing something once.

Our Mission

We aim to make second-hand fashion the first choice for everyone. By blending sustainability with style, Threads & Roses empowers conscious shoppers to find high-quality, pre-loved pieces that make a statement ‚Äî for you and the planet.
